Lasagna

Description

Indulge in the mouthwatering flavors of this traditional and comforting Classic Lasagna. Layers of tender pasta, rich tomato sauce, savory meat, and creamy cheese come together to create a hearty and satisfying dish that will leave your taste buds dancing with delight. Whether you're cooking for a family dinner or entertaining guests, this recipe is sure to be a crowd-pleaser.

Ingredients

  • 12 lasagna noodles
  • 1 lb ground beef or Italian sausage
  • 1 medium onion, finely chopped
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 can crushed tomatoes
  • 1 can tomato sauce
  • 2 tablespoons tomato paste
  • 2 teaspoons dried basil
  • 2 teaspoons dried oregano
  • 1 teapsoon sugar
  • Salt and pepper
  • 1 egg
  • 15 oz ricotta cheese
  • 1 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • Fresh parsley, chopped

Steps

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C). Grease a 9x13-inch baking dish with non-stick spray or butter.
  2. In a large skillet or saucepan, brown the ground beef (or sausage) over medium-high heat. Add chopped onions and garlic, cooking until they become translucent and fragrant.
  3. Stir in the crushed tomatoes, tomato sauce, tomato paste, dried basil, dried oregano, sugar, salt, and pepper. Let the sauce simmer on low heat for about 20-25 minutes to allow the flavors to meld together.
  4. In a separate bowl, mix together the ricotta cheese, egg, and half of the grated Parmesan cheese. Add a pinch of salt and pepper to taste.
  5. Now, it's time to assemble the lasagna! Spread a thin layer of the meat sauce on the bottom of the greased baking dish. Arrange a layer of lasagna noodles over the sauce.
  6. Next, spread a generous layer of the ricotta mixture over the noodles, followed by a layer of shredded mozzarella cheese.
  7. Repeat the layers: meat sauce, noodles, ricotta mixture, and mozzarella cheese. Continue until all the ingredients are used up, finishing with a layer of meat sauce and a generous sprinkle of mozzarella and Parmesan cheeses on top.
  8. Cover the baking dish with aluminum foil, ensuring it's tightly sealed to keep the moisture in.
  9. Bake the lasagna in the preheated oven for 25 minutes. Then, remove the foil and continue baking for an additional 20-25 minutes, or until the cheese on top is bubbly and lightly golden.
  10. Once done, take the lasagna out of the oven and let it rest for a few minutes before serving. Garnish with chopped fresh parsley for a burst of color and freshness.
